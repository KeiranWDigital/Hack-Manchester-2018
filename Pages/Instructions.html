Welcome to Travel Badger!


Finding the tram a bit bright and noisy? Bored? Want to know a bit more about Manchester? Never fear! Travel Badger is here to help you!

The idea is simple - as you travel around, check out the map and click on a badger. Each one will lead you to a new discovery about the city around us.

Every so often you'll unlock a new badge(r) - see if you can collect the sett ;)
